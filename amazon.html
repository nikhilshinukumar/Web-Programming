Amazon

Amazon is the next best online shopping site in India. Amazon has a large customer base almost as much as Flipkart. There is always a war between Amazon and Flipkart. The gap between the market share of Amazon and Flipkart' is very low. Just like Flipkart, Amazon also offers a huge range of products. While homegrown online retailer Flipkart leads in fashion and smartphones, Amazon has taken the lead in categories like appliances, consumer electronics, and groceries.

Amazon sells a huge range of products including books, electronic goods, cosmetics, footwear, consumer durables, clothes and much more. Along with all this, Amazon also sells grocery products at a reasonable rate. To attract consumers, Amazon offers a wide range of discounts during the festive season.
