TOURIST SPOTS

edakkal caves

edakkal caves also edakal, are two natural caves at edakkal in wayanad district of kerala.The caves are situated 1200 m above sea level on Ambukutty mala, on the mysore plateau, in the western ghats.

soochipara waterfalls

soochipara waterfalls also known as sentinel rock waterfalls is a three-tiered waterfall in vellarimala,wayanad,india.It is surrounded by deciduous,evergreen and montane forests.

kuruvadweep

kuruvadweep or kuruva island is a 950-acre protected river delta. It comprises three densely wooded uninhabited islands and a few submergible satellite islands,which lies on the banks of the tributaries of kabini river in the wayanad district.
