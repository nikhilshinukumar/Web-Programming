Snapdeal

oftbank-backed Snapdeal is India's third largest online shopping site. It is a favorite among many Indians as it sells products at a cheap price. Snapdeal sells more than 35 million products across more than 800 categories from over 125,000 regional, national, and international brands and retailers. It has more than 3,00,000 sellers and delivers to 6000+ cities and towns in India.

Snapdeal which is based in New Delhi was started in the year 2010 as a daily deals platform, but later became an online marketplace in September 2011.
